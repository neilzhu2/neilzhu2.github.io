ABOUT

Jicun Village is a village of long history and is located beside another famous tourist attraction named Hongcun Village.

The development in Jicun, however, is in chaos and of low efficiency. Buildings with mediocre looking are constructed everywhere in this village. While the characterized sightseeings are hidden behind, which would be hard to find for the visitors.

In addition, travellers in the narrow street may also cause inconvenience to the local people’s lives.

In order to solve the two issues, we decide to design a route beyond the ground.
