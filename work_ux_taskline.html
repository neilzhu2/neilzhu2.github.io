TaskLine

I collaborated with two friends with the same enthusiasm in HCI and this is my first UX design project.
It provides an app to help task management by visualiztion.
I was the project leader, but we shared a lot of work.

UX Design _ APP
2016.11-2017.01
Partners: Shichao Wang/ Rujun Huang
My Work: Literature Review/ Competitor Analysis/ User Research/ Persona/ Brainstorm/ Affinity Diagram/ Prototype Design/ Usability Test/ Iteration

PROBLEM

People with numerous tasks in various categories, like students, tend to feel anxious and impetuous accordingly, which leads them to anxiously act just for catch deadlines, and become tired both emotionally and physically, rather than rationally analyzing and making plans to finish tasks with efficiency as well as quality.
